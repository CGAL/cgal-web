A Special Event for a Double Anniversary

The year 2026 will see two major milestones for CGAL: the 30th anniversary of the project, and the 50th CGAL developer meeting!

To celebrate this, we will append a special event to the next developer meeting to get together and meet people who have been involved with CGAL over the years, to share memories, stories, thoughts, feelings and opinions, both personal and concerning the past, present, and future of the project that connects us all: CGAL.

The event is intended to be mostly informal and social, with lots of opportunities for individual interaction. But we also plan to have a short program, with presentations and panel discussions on CGAL, computational geometry, and open source projects. There are a few items on the program already, and we do not intend to fully "program" both days of the event. Nevertheless, there is still room for contributions. So please do not hesitate to contact the organizers at anniversary@cgal.org if you would like to contribute.


Schedule and Venue

Where: Inria Centre at Université Côte d'Azur, Sophia Antipolis (France)

When: April 20th-25th, 2026

Developer Meeting: April 20th-22nd, 2026

A "regular" CGAL developer meeting will take place from Monday to Wednesday. This meeting is intended for active CGAL developers and maintainers, but others are welcome to join as well.

Anniversary Event: April 23rd-24th, 2026

The anniversary event will take place on Thursday and Friday. The presentations and discussions will start on Thursday afternoon as to allow participants to arrive on Thursday morning. The event will conclude on Friday afternoon. The detailed program will be announced in due time on this page.

On Thursday evening there will be a cocktail reception at INRIA, and on Friday evening a dinner party to celebrate the anniversary.

Saturday Outing: April 25th, 2026

On Saturday April 25th, 2026, we plan to have an outing in the area around Nice.


Logistics

The costs for travel and accommodation have to be covered by you. Cocktail and dinner party will be covered by the organizers.
