A Special Event for a Double Anniversary

The year 2026 will see two major milestones for CGAL: the 30th anniversary of the project, and the 50th CGAL developer meeting!

To celebrate this, we have invited people involved over the years to to join us during the next developer meeting to share memories, stories and thoughts over the project that connect us all: CGAL.

The anniversary of the CGAL project was also the occasion to reflect upon the structure of the project, the reasons behind its longetivity, and how should the future look like. To this end, we have organized a (two, in fact!) special event dedicated to open source projects and invited notable, big and small, neighboring open source projects such that they may talk about themselves, and we can discuss what works, and what does not.

These events are intended to be social, with lots of opportunities for individual interaction. We also have left plenty of space for suggestions on other days of the week. So please do not hesitate to contact the organizers at anniversary@cgal.org if you would like to suggest something.


Schedule and Venue

Where: Inria Centre at Université Côte d'Azur, Sophia Antipolis (France)

When: April 20th-25th, 2026

Developer Meeting: April 20th-24nd, 2026

A "regular" CGAL developer meeting will take place from Monday to Friday, except when special events take place. This meeting is intended for active CGAL developers and maintainers, but others are welcome to join as well.

Special Events: April 23rd, 2026

Two special events will take place on Thursday: technical showcases of three major geometric open-source libraries, and a panel discussion in the afternoon focused on the organizational aspects of Open Source projects. The full program is available on this dedicated page.

On Thursday evening there will be a cocktail reception at Inria, and on Friday evening a dinner party at the Nice Observatory to celebrate the anniversary.


Saturday Outing: April 25th, 2026

On Saturday April 25th, 2026, we plan to have an outing in the area around Nice, with multiple options depending on the weather.


Logistics

The costs for travel and accommodation have to be covered by you. Cocktail and dinner party will be covered by the organizers.
